## Executive summary

This is a **public-evidence** due-diligence review of **Able.cz s.r.o.**
(IČO 24278815), a Brno software company. It separates what the public record
independently verifies from what the company reports about itself. It is
**not** a full transaction due diligence: no data room, management interviews,
contracts, bank records, tax files, or source-code access were available, and
public evidence cannot substitute for them.

The company's core identity is **verified** against the public register. Most of
its marketing and performance claims are **self-reported** and await independent
corroboration — the chart below shows that distribution directly. One material
**contradiction** was found and is left visible rather than smoothed over: the
company's own legal page and the public register disagree on the court file
number. Filed financial statements were **not retrieved** in this pass, so no
revenue, profit, or headcount figure is asserted here.

Confidence is **moderate**: high on identity, low on the performance claims,
with real gaps flagged throughout.

## Scope, methodology & correction policy

Evidence is labelled by status: **VERIFIED_PRIMARY** (a primary source),
**CORROBORATED** (two independent sources), **SELF_REPORTED** (Able's own
statement), **ASSESSED** (analytical reading), **CONTRADICTED** (sources
disagree), and **NOT_FOUND** (searched, unresolved). These are not collapsed
into a single score. Absence of public evidence is an evidence gap, not proof of
anything. Every figure below traces to the source ledger at the foot of the
page. Corrections are welcome and will be dated.

## Identity — *Verified*

Able.cz s.r.o. is a limited-liability company incorporated on **3 October 2012**,
registered at the Regional Court in Brno, with its seat at **Vlněna 526/5,
Trnitá, 602 00 Brno**, IČO **24278815**, DIČ **CZ24278815**, and registered
capital of **243,715 CZK**. Each of these is verified against the public
register; the full field-by-field table with sources appears below.

## The register contradiction — *Contradicted*

The company's own GDPR page cites court file **C 85424**; the public register
records **C 85425**. The register is authoritative, so C 85425 is the operative
value and the page figure is most plausibly a transcription error or stale copy.
It is recorded here rather than silently corrected, because a due-diligence
document that hides the discrepancies it finds is worth less than one that shows
them.

## Governance — *Verified, with a distinction*

The register lists the statutory body as **Václav Faraga** and **Tomáš
Melichárek** (both since incorporation), with **Radek Juhaňák** among the
shareholders. Acts above **200,000 CZK** require the statutory officers to sign
jointly — a real internal control, verifiable in the register.

The website markets four leaders, including a **CTO (Lukáš Grolig)** and **CSO
(Radek Juhaňák)**. A marketing job title is not a statutory office: Grolig was
not found in the register's statutory body or shareholder list in this pass. The
graph below shows each person's relationship to the company and the evidence
behind it.

## Public claims — *Mostly self-reported*

Able publishes an extensive set of performance claims — 150+ in-house AI agents,
300% average client ROI, >90% error reduction, and several quantified client
results. As the status chart shows, **14 of the 15 material claims reviewed are
self-reported**: published by the company, not independently confirmed. A named
client is evidence that a relationship was stated, not that a quantified result
occurred; marketing copy is evidence of positioning, not of capability. The one
**corroborated** item is the 2025 acquisition of the product studio **Blackfish**,
reported consistently by two independent outlets.

## Financial profile — *Largely not found*

The only hard financial figure obtained from a primary source is the registered
capital (**243,715 CZK**). Czech companies file their statements in the
Collection of Deeds (Sbírka listin) at justice.cz; those filings were **not
retrieved** in this pass, so revenue, result, assets, and headcount are marked
NOT_FOUND rather than estimated. A media-reported combined revenue **target** of
~100M CZK (Able plus Blackfish) is self-reported and forward-looking, not a
filed result.
