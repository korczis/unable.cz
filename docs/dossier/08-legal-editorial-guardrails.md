# 08 — Legal & editorial guardrails

## Authorization
The dossier's subject (a named third party) is permitted **only** because the
site owner authorized it explicitly and on the record on 2026-07-17 (AGENTS.md →
*Content about real parties*). No other named third party is in scope.

## The container risk
The site premise — *"We are UNABLE To PAY U"* — is a negation-pun. A
due-diligence-framed page about a named company could imply something about its
solvency before a reader reads a word. The dossier neutralizes this by:
- an explicit disclaimer that it asserts **no insolvency, wrongdoing, or
  inability to pay**, and that none was found;
- keeping the subject's identity and the site's satire clearly separated;
- labelling every statement by evidence status.

## Rules applied
- Publish no accusation as fact; no statements about intent ("refused to pay",
  "hiding assets") — none are made.
- Prefer precise wording: "X was reported by source Y as of date Z"; "the public
  record reviewed did not establish …"; "no insolvency proceeding was identified
  in the reviewed sources as of the cutoff — this does not prove solvency."
- Distinguish: absence of evidence ≠ evidence of absence ≠ allegation ≠
  confirmed event ≠ opinion ≠ inference.
- Mask personal sensitive data (private phones, home addresses, birth numbers,
  private emails). Only public-register identity facts are published.

## Corrections & limitations
A visible methodology, limitations, and disclaimer are on the page. Corrections
go to `corrections@unable.cz` (`CORRECTIONS.md`) and are dated. No inference is a
judicial finding.
