Executive summary

unable.cz is a single-page satirical site whose headline asserts inability to pay while simultaneously asserting capability. This page separates what can be independently verified about the site from what the site merely claims about itself.

Scope & methodology

This assessment is limited to the public unable.cz page and its build configuration, as observed on 2026-07-16. No external financial, legal, or corporate registry was consulted, because no evidence ties this domain to a formal legal entity of that name. Every statement below should be read with that limitation in mind.

Identity & footprint — Observed

  • The domain resolves via a CNAME record to a GitHub Pages deployment.
  • The site is generated by Zola from a single Markdown source, styled with Tailwind CSS, Flowbite, and Flowbite Typography.
  • Alpine.js is loaded on every page; no element currently depends on it for content or navigation.
  • No analytics script, CMS, server process, or database is present as of this writing.

Confidence: high. Each item above is independently verifiable by inspecting the deployed page and its build output.

Claimed positioning — Claimed

The headline states: "We are UNABLE to PAY U — Just Because We CAN." No services, pricing, or contact information are asserted anywhere on the page.

The central contradiction — Assessed

The claim of inability sits directly beside a claim of latent capability. Read literally, "unable to pay" and "choosing not to" are mutually exclusive — an entity that cannot pay has no choice in the matter, while an entity that chooses not to pay is, by definition, able. The most plausible reading is that this is a poster-style paradox, not a financial disclosure.

Confidence: high that this is the intended rhetorical device rather than a literal statement of insolvency.

Messaging analysis — Assessed

The pairing of UNABLE / PAY U / CAN compresses "unable" and "you" across the three lines, load-bearing on typography and line breaks rather than on argument. It reads as wordplay, not as a claim requiring evidence.

Open questions — Unknown

  • Whether unable.cz refers to a legal entity, an individual, a running joke, or a domain acquired purely as a typographic artwork.
  • Whether the page's content will change over time.

Public evidence available at the time of writing does not resolve either question.
